Winter in Pakistan

Murree

During winter, Murree temperature is very low, its near to -1 centigrade, and mostly snowfall starts during these seasons.

Hunza

In winter, there will be no traces of greenery can be found in Hunza and the whole region remains under the snow curtain.

Gilgit

Gilgit-Baltistan is known to be the coldest region of Pakistan. The lowest temperature recorded there is -35 deg cen winter.

Winter in USA

Syracruse

On coldest winter nights at Syracruse, thermometer drops to 0°F(-18°C) . On 47 days a year, temperature here never rises above freezing.

Alpena

Alpena in the winter is especially pretty, surrounded by soft white snow and glistening ice. Surrounded by the Great Lakes, rivers, and natural forests.

Boise

Winters in Boise are moderately cold the average daily high temperature is below 47°F. Coldest day of the year tends to be around January 1, with an average low of 24°F.

Winter in Switzerland

Zermatt

January through December are months with snowfall in Zermatt. In Zermatt, snow falls for 13.9 days and regularly aggregates up to 220mm (8.66") of snow.

St Moritz

The snow forecast for St Moritz is: Mostly dry. Temperatures will be below freezing (max -3°C min -11°C on Mon night). Wind will be generally light.
